Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No long-term contracts required. Our Business and Enterprise plans are billed monthly and you can cancel anytime.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and direct bank transfers for Enterprise plans.
