Pricing FAQ

How does the commission structure work?

The 2% commission applies only to bids that come through the online platform. There's no commission on floor bids, phone bids, or other channels that you manage separately.

Is there a contract or commitment period?

Both plans operate on a month-to-month basis with no long-term contract required. You can cancel at any time.

How does the money-back guarantee work?

If you decide Circuit Auction isn't right for your business within the first 45 days, simply contact us and we'll process a full refund of all fees paid.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can switch between the Standard and Enterprise plans with 30 days' notice at the start of your next billing cycle.

Are there any setup or implementation fees?

No. Setup, implementation, and standard training are included at no additional cost with both plans.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and direct bank transfers for both plans.
