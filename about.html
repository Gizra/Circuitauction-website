About Circuit Auction

We're transforming the auction industry with innovative technology designed specifically for collectibles dealers.

Our Story

Circuit Auction was born from a deep understanding of both the collecting world and technology. Founded by a team that combines decades of auction experience with cutting-edge technical expertise, we set out to solve the unique challenges faced by auction houses in the digital age.

What began as a solution for a single auction house has evolved into a comprehensive platform serving collectibles dealers across the globe. We recognized that while the collectibles market was growing rapidly, the technology supporting it hadn't kept pace with modern expectations.

Our founders' backgrounds in philately, numismatics, and software development created the perfect foundation for building a platform that truly understands the nuances of specialty auctions. We've worked closely with auction houses of all sizes to develop features that address real-world challenges in catalog creation, bidder engagement, and auction management.

Our Mission & Values

Innovation
We continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in auction technology, developing new solutions that enhance the experience for both auction houses and collectors.
Partnership
We see ourselves as partners, not just providers. Your success is our success, which is why we're committed to supporting your growth and adapting to your unique needs.
Integrity
We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all we do, from secure handling of bidder information to transparent pricing. We build trust through honest business practices.
