Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our auction management platform. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

What is Circuit Auction and how is it different from other auction software?

Circuit Auction is a comprehensive all-in-one platform built specifically for auction houses. It combines cataloging, client management, bidding, invoicing, and shipping into a single interface. Unlike generic solutions, it is fully tailored to the needs of auction professionals.

Can I brand the platform with my auction house's identity?

Absolutely. You can customize the entire interface — from logo and colors to layout — and run it under your own domain. Your clients experience a seamless, branded environment.

How can I create an auction catalog quickly and professionally?

Our AI-powered assistant helps you build catalogs in minutes by generating descriptions, organizing categories, and estimating values based on historical data.

Does the platform support multiple languages?

Yes. The frontend and backend support multiple languages, allowing your staff and international bidders to work in their native language.

Is there a way to track changes made to auction lots?

Yes, the Revision Control System logs every change and allows you to restore previous versions or see who edited what and when.

How does image handling work?

With Amazon S3 integration, you can bulk upload images and let the system automatically match them to lots based on filenames.

How quickly can auction houses implement Circuit?

It's a five-minute process — just install the plugin and you're ready to go.

Can I manage client relationships inside the platform?

Yes. The built-in CRM tracks client history, preferences, and communication, enabling personalized outreach and higher engagement.

How do I organize lots and apply numbering rules?

Flexible lot numbering systems allow custom sequences and grouping that align with your house's practices.

Can I scan items with barcodes?

Yes. Barcode integration streamlines intake and tracking, reducing manual entry errors and speeding up workflows.

Is the system adaptable to my workflows?

You can configure custom workflows, approval processes, and conditional actions to fit your internal processes.

Can I edit multiple items at once?

Yes, batch update tools let you apply changes to dozens or hundreds of lots simultaneously.

How does consignment management work?

Track each consignment from intake to settlement, auto-generate contracts, and offer real-time status updates to consignors.

Yes. Store certificates, provenance, and expert opinions and generate downloadable authentication reports.

Yes. Pre-sale bids transition seamlessly into live events, with synchronized updates across desktop and mobile.

Can I set bidding limits per client?

Yes. Limits can be defined based on credit checks, deposits, or client history.

Yes. Bids from email, mail, or third-party systems can be imported and consolidated within the platform.

Invoices are automatically created post-auction, with taxes, fees, and shipping included. Batch sending is supported.

Yes. The system calculates costs and produces labels based on item details and buyer preferences.
